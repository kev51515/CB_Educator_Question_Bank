Q6 60000

The correct answer is 60,000. It’s given that the weight of sample A is 468% of the weight of sample B and 0.780% of the weight of sample C. Let the weight of sample B be b and the weight of sample C be c. Then, the weight of sample A is equivalent to 468b100, or 4.68b, and also equivalent to 0.780c100, or 0.00780c. Setting these expressions equal to each other yields 4.68b=0.00780c. Dividing both sides of this equation by 0.00780 yields 4.68b0.00780=c, or c=600b. If the weight of sample C is p% of the weight of sample B, then c=p 100b. Since c=600b and c=p 100b, it follows that 600b=p 100b, or 600=p100. Multiplying both sides of this equation by 100 yields 60,000=p. Therefore, the value of p is 60,000.
